Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

One Last Move Studio develops and publishes the mobile game Marble5. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use the application.

Data Collection

Marble5 does not collect any personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Any other personal information

All your game data (progress, scores) is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Marble5 uses Google Play Games Services for leaderboard features. This service is managed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

When you use the leaderboards, Google may collect information in accordance with its own policy. We do not have access to this data.

For any questions about Google Play distribution, contact us.

Advertising

The Marble5 private beta contains no advertising.

In-App Purchases

The Marble5 private beta is 100% free and offers no in-app purchases.

Children

Marble5 is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us through our website: marble5.app
